About

Privacy, reimagined

UKULELE is a privacy-first brand built around a simple conviction: confidentiality should be the default, not a feature you have to hunt for. This is the story of a rebrand — a stronger identity for a clearer mission.

The mission

We believe privacy is freedom. In a world where digital life is tracked by default, UKULELE stands for the opposite — a brand and community that puts the individual back in control of their own information. Everything we design, from the mark to the messaging, is meant to make that idea feel approachable and human.

Why the ukulele?

The ukulele is small, friendly, and open — an instrument anyone can pick up. Wrapped in a protective ring with a keyhole at its heart, our logo turns that warmth into a symbol of guarded, personal expression. It's privacy that doesn't feel cold or clinical. It feels like something you'd actually want to hold.

What we stand for

Private by default. Secure at the core. Decentralized in spirit. These aren't taglines bolted on after the fact — they're the constraints the whole project is built around. As the community grows, the mission stays the same: make privacy something everyone can understand, use, and believe in.
