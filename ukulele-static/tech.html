Technology

A foundation for privacy

The design principles that guide how UKULELE thinks about building responsibly — a solid, scalable foundation with privacy and security treated as first-class requirements.

Privacy by design

Confidentiality is considered from the first line of the spec, not patched in later. Systems that collect less, expose less.

Security first

Defensive engineering, careful review, and a preference for simple, auditable designs over clever complexity.

Open & decentralized

Transparency where it counts. Community governance and open participation over closed control.

Scalable & sustainable

Built to grow gracefully — a foundation that stays maintainable as the community and its needs expand.

Responsible by default

Privacy technology carries real responsibility. UKULELE frames its work around lawful, legitimate use — protecting ordinary people's data and personal autonomy — and around building tools that are transparent about what they do. The full technical approach is documented in the whitepaper, which the community is invited to read, question, and help improve.
